The SexBomb Girls (also known as SB Girls or SBG) are a Filipino girl group formed in 1999 by Joy Cancio. They first gained popularity as dancers on Eat Bulaga! before expanding into singing and acting. In the early 2000s, they became one of the most successful P-pop groups in the Philippines, releasing multiple studio albums that achieved multi-platinum sales. In 2007, they were inducted into the Box Office Entertainment Awards Hall of Fame after winning five consecutive “Most Popular Dance Group” awards. The group remains an iconic part of early-2000s Filipino pop culture.
